This project contributes to a National Natural Science Foundation of China general program on spatial ability assessment through video games. I served as the main developer of an educational game for children's spatial reasoning, with interactive 3D construction tasks, adaptive difficulty, and built-in assessment.

The system explores how game mechanics can make spatial reasoning visible, measurable, and trainable in learning contexts.
