At Hailiang Education, I delivered outreach lectures on popular science and learning methods. These sessions were designed for young learners and public audiences who needed educational content that was clear, encouraging, and connected to everyday experience.

Rather than presenting science as a list of facts, I tried to make learning methods visible: how to ask better questions, how to break down difficult topics, how to practice deliberately, and how to stay curious when school learning becomes exam-centered.
