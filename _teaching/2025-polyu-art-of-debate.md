As a teaching assistant for Art of Debate at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, I supported students in developing argumentation, public speaking, and structured discussion skills. The course connects closely with my broader interest in spoken language practice: effective communication involves timing, evidence, confidence, audience awareness, and social presence.

My work involved supporting classroom activities, helping students prepare debate materials, and providing feedback on the clarity and persuasiveness of their arguments.
