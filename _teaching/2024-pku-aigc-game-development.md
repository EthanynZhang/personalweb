As a student lecturer at Geek Lab of Peking University, I taught Unity, AIGC, and project-based creative technology to students interested in games, interactive media, and AI-assisted production. The course was designed around hands-on making: students learned core game-engine workflows, explored generative AI tools, and gradually turned early ideas into playable or demonstrable prototypes.

My role combined technical instruction and project mentoring. I helped students understand how to move from a vague creative concept to a working interactive system, how to debug Unity scenes, and how to use AI tools responsibly as part of a design workflow.
