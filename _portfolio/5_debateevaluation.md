DebateEvaluation is an AI-assisted platform project for debate-oriented and programming-related learning activities. The system was built around the CodeShell model from the National Research Center for Software Engineering of Peking University.

The project explores how AI systems can provide structured support for argumentation, feedback, and learning workflows in computational education contexts.
