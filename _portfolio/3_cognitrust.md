CogniTrust is an experimental HCI project on how affective cues in social media comments influence user trust and decision-making under information distortion. The project uses an interactive decision-making system to examine how emotional cues change information processing, confidence, and perceived credibility.

The broader aim is to understand how interface-level social signals can shape judgment in complex media environments, especially when users must reason under uncertainty.
