This project develops an XR educational experience that combines game mechanics with archaeological inquiry. Learners interact with spatial evidence, reconstruct context, and reason through historical or scientific problems in an immersive environment.

The design goal is to support spatial reasoning and critical thinking through active exploration rather than passive content delivery. The project connects XR learning design, game-based learning, inquiry-based pedagogy, and assessment of spatial cognition.
